Krypton
Atomic Number: 36

Atomic Mass: 83.798

Electronegativity: — (noble gas, very low reactivity)

Density: 0.003749 g/cm³

Melting Point: 115.8 K

Boiling Point: 119.9 K

Electron Config: [Ar] 3d¹⁰ 4s² 4p⁶

Discovery

Krypton was discovered in 1898 by William Ramsay and Morris Travers in London.

Krypton has several stable isotopes, including ⁷⁸Kr, ⁸⁰Kr, ⁸²Kr, ⁸³Kr, ⁸⁴Kr, and ⁸⁶Kr.

Uses

- Lighting (high-intensity lamps)
- Lasers (krypton-ion lasers)
- Insulation in windows
- Research in nuclear physics

Fun Fact

Krypton’s name comes from the Greek word “kryptos”, meaning “hidden.”
