Ag
Silver
Atomic Number: 47

Atomic Mass: 107.868

Electronegativity: 1.93

Density: 10.49 g/cm³

Melting Point: 1234 K

Boiling Point: 2435 K

Electron Config: [Kr] 4d¹⁰ 5s¹

Discovery

Silver has been known since antiquity and used by many ancient civilizations.

Isotopes

Silver has two stable isotopes: ¹⁰⁷Ag and ¹⁰⁹Ag.

Uses

- Jewelry and coins
- Electrical conductors
- Photography (silver halides)
- Medical applications (antibacterial)

Fun Fact

Silver has the highest electrical and thermal conductivity of all metals.
